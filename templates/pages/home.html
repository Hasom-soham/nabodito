Our thoughts

From our secretary desk

It gives me immense pleasure to introduce ourselves as an NGO named ‘BARANAGAR NABODITO’ which is primarily focused to ensure a nominal development of our society especially backward and needy area. We, the people, as well as member of this organization has already taken some steps to enlighten the society like some needy family with an enrichment of those people’s daily life by kind though our little work till now is like a dew in an ocean of social work. We know it very well but we know also that the word of work limitations is completely value less in the field of social work. We would not like to know about our limitations but we believe if we are dare to overcome our any types of restriction then we can get an achievement like to take one step to the path of success. It would be better to believe that in the ocean of social work, success itself is a continuing process. We are abiding by our rule for this continuation. A decade ago, ‘BARANAGAR NABODITO’ started its journey with the greetings and love as well as blessings from so many friends and well-wishers which was just beyond our expectations. From 2012 to 2022 we have visited real backward village of West Bengal for distributing cloths to needy children. Even in 2020, the black covid19 period of the world, we reached Birinchibari village in South 24 Pargana district under Basanti Police Station. We arrange blood donation camp in every year in our premises, also we invite eye specialist Doctor for free eye test of our beloved residents of the area of Barangar. Time speaks. The work of the organization does not stop, nor will it. However, it is hoped that with time the scope of work of the organization will increase and the work of the organization will be handed over to a worthy successor. The organization will find the successor in its own interest. Love and Regards to all. Jay Hind.

Program of the last decade

Before summarizing the program let's define the purpose of the organization. There is no need for an organization to benefit a single individual but when people are working for the benefit of society, there is a need for a collective effort which is greater than a single action and this is when an organization is needed which is able to serve the purpose of many . The main objective here is to bring the socially and financially backward sections of the society under the limelight. If this objective is the target, then the organization can be said to have taken a little step towards the objective. However, he is determined to stay steady and steady on the way to his goal, just needs some helping hand

  • In 2012, Baranagar Nabodito tried to put a smile on the faces of street children in Kolkata, at least for a moment, by distributing new clothes to them. In subsequent years, the organization reached out to various districts of West Bengal, particularly the economically backward areas and tribal-dominated areas.
  • In 2013, Baranagar Nabodito expanded its operations outside Kolkata. That year the organization reached Birinchi Bari village of Sonarpur police station in South 24 Parganas district on the eve of autumn Durga Puja. There the boys and girls were given new clothes which were given to them as puja festival gifts.
  • In 2014, the organization reached Khodahati village under Sonarpur police station and Hatchara village under Joynagar police station of South 24 Parganas district. On the eve of Durga Puja, about 300 children's clothes were measured and the effort continued to make new clothes and hand them over.
  • In 2015, the organization reached Paraj rural area under Galsi police station in Burdwan district PICTURE
  • In 2016, the destination of Baranagar Nabadito organization reached Harinhula village under Minakha Block and Police Station of North Twenty Four Parganas District.
  • In 2017, The Organization reached Bhogpur village under Debra Police station of west Medinipur District.
  • In 2018, the organization reached the villages of Aralkocha, Kulgora, Asanbani, Mor jangalpur and Palashkala in Purulia district. The boys and girls there are given new clothes advent of Durga Puja festival.
  • In 2019, the organization reached Chatuhas and Ashra No. 1 villages of Purulia district.
  • In 2020, when the entire world came to a standstill due to the fear of the Covid virus, Baranagar Nabodito did not stop this program. This year Baranagar Nabodito went Birinchibari village once again.
  • Since 2021, the organization has been reaching Pitidhiri, Gundalia, Bnadhodi and Ayodhya village under Bagmundi police station of Purulia district. New clothes are being given to the needy, poor tribal children, the elderly and boys and girls, even winter clothes have also been delivered in winter.
  • In 2022, Bharat Sevashram Sangh Chief Maharaj has auspiciously started the Ananda Utsav by delivering clothes on the eve of Puja in the same region.
  • Other Works
  • 1. Every year Blood Donation camp
  • 2.Free Eye test, Blood pressure and sugar test of local citizens.

Apart from the health aspect of citizens, the organization has always recognized that sports is a part of life and a condition of good health and has encouraged the youth in this regard. It has organized a three day Swami Vivekananda youth football competition. PICTURE The organization established a newspaper stand for local inhabitants.
